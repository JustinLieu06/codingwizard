//GAME ENTITIES

Coding Wizard


Score: 0
INSTRUCTIONS

Overview:

You are playing as a coding wizard who casts powerful programming magic to defeat the bugs in your developed universe.

Controls:

Press WASD to move the wizard up, left, down, and right respectively.

Click to cast a fireball spell.

Your spell's direction are based off of where you click in relation to the wizard.

Scoring:

Changing a tile increases the score.

Killing a bug increases the score

Having a bug reach you decreases the score.
